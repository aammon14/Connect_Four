CONNECT FOUR

How to play:

In this game of Connect Four, two players alternate turns dropping their respective discs into the game board. To drop a disc into the game board, click on the column you would like your disc to fall in. Once dropped, the disc will fill to the lowest available space in that column.

The first person to get four discs lined up in a row either vertically, horizontally, or diagonally wins the game.
